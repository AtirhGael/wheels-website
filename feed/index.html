The Forged Advantage: Real Weight Savings & Strength

Unlike cast or even flow-formed wheels, BBS forged wheels are created under extreme pressure from a single billet of high-grade aluminum (or advanced alloys). This process aligns the grain structure for exceptional strength while allowing aggressive weight reduction through back-milling and precise design.
